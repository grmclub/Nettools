𝗣𝗿𝗼𝗯𝗹𝗲𝗺:
Identify all missing integers within a non-continuous sequence of IDs from a table.

𝗧𝗵𝗲 𝗚𝗲𝗻𝗲𝗿𝗮𝘁𝗲 & 𝗙𝗶𝗹𝘁𝗲𝗿 𝗣𝗮𝘁𝘁𝗲𝗿𝗻:
01. 𝗜𝗱𝗲𝗻𝘁𝗶𝗳𝘆: Find the MIN (1) and MAX (10) boundaries from your table.
02. 𝗚𝗲𝗻𝗲𝗿𝗮𝘁𝗲: Create a complete, gapless sequence of all numbers between those boundaries.
03. 𝗙𝗶𝗹𝘁𝗲𝗿: Keep only the records that do NOT exist in the original table.

This pattern is a "Swiss Army Knife" for auditing:
- 𝗘𝗺𝗽𝗹𝗼𝘆𝗲𝗲 𝗜𝗗𝘀: Find skipped student or staff records.
- 𝗠𝗶𝘀𝘀𝗶𝗻𝗴 𝗗𝗮𝘁𝗲𝘀: Identify gaps in time-series reports.
- 𝗢𝗿𝗱𝗲𝗿 𝗔𝘂𝗱𝗶𝘁𝘀: Ensure no gaps in sequential order numbers.
- 𝗦𝘆𝘀𝘁𝗲𝗺 𝗟𝗼𝗴𝘀: Detect gaps in sequential log entries.
