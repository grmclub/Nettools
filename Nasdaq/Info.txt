* Converting timestamp offset from Midnight (market data/order entry)

1. Get epoch timestamp at midnight
    #date -d "2013-11-21 00:00:00" +%s

2. Add Time to epoch & convert
    #date -d @$((1384959600 + 45443))
    Thu Nov 21 12:37:23 JST 2013
